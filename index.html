WELCOME, COMMANDER

This is the full operator manual for Stone's Portal Generator.

Tap NEXT to flip through the cards, or BACK to review. Close anytime with the × above.

Tap NEXT to begin
QUICK START
Four steps to your first portal
1

Tap the big green portal in the preview to upload your PFP.

2

Drag the image to pan. Pinch to zoom inside the ring.

3

Customize rings and colors using the tabs at the bottom.

4

Hit the SAVE tab, pick a resolution, tap SAVE PNG, download.

THE FIVE TABS
What's in each one
IMAGE
Upload, swap, or clear your PFP. Control zoom into the image.
RINGS
Ring count (1-4), per-ring widths, and per-ring colors.
STYLE
Glow mode, background, XM∑M∑ text ring size and position.
MORE
Preset slots, reset button, system console.
SAVE
Pick resolution, share to X, view export history.
RINGS (1 to 4)
Stack and customize

Use the + and − buttons to stack up to 4 rings around your PFP.

Each ring gets its own width slider (1-80px) AND its own color swatch.

Tap the color square next to any ring to change it to any color on the full color wheel.

Ring 1 is the outermost. The last ring is the innermost (labeled INNER).

Adjust SPACING to control the gap between rings.

XM∑M∑ TEXT RING
The branded text wrap

The gold panel right under the preview toggles the XM∑M∑ text ring ON or OFF.

Tap the chevron to expand the size and position controls.

TEXT SIZE
Bigger = fewer, bolder repeats. Smaller = tight crispy lettering.
POSITION
OUTER wraps text beyond all rings. R1, R2, R3, R4 place text on a specific ring (that ring hides automatically).
STYLE OPTIONS
Glow & background
GLOW MODES
STEADY — smooth clean rings
PULSE — radar halos radiating out
WAVE — sinusoidal wave rings
FLICKER — broken neon segments
NONE — flat, no glow
BACKGROUND
TRANSPARENT — default, best for PFPs
SOLID — one color fill
GRADIENT — radial depth
SAVING & EXPORTS
1K, 2K, 4K downloads

Pick 1K / 2K / 4K from the SAVE tab or inside the save modal. Your choice sticks.

On mobile, long-press the preview image to save straight to Photos.

The DOWNLOAD button stays disabled until the file is ready. Spinner icon = still rendering.

SHIP TO OPTI?
Check this box in the save modal to also archive your export to the Opti Discord channel. Optional, off by default.
HISTORY & PRESETS
Save & re-use
EXPORT HISTORY
Your last 6 exports are saved on your device.
Survives page reloads and closed browsers.
Tap any thumbnail to re-open and re-download at ANY resolution.
CLEAR button wipes all saved exports.
PRESETS (MORE TAB)
3 slots for favorite ring & color combos.
Select a slot → SAVE SLOT → LOAD SLOT later.
PRO TIPS
Get the most out of it
  • Thin inner rings (2-4px) with chunky outer rings look slick.
  • PULSE and WAVE glow modes each give the rings a totally different look — try them all.
  • Mix contrasting colors — gold outer with green inner hits different.
  • Tap the flashing at the top for BGM. Volume slider + play/pause in the floating bar.
  • XRPMAN reacts to every button you tap. Try the glow modes or ring +/-.
  • Re-open this guide anytime with the in the top-LEFT corner.
